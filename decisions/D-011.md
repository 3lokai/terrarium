# D-011 — The lineage is witnessable: a per-day archive and a timeline you can scrub
**Day 005 · 2026-06-18 · ACTIVE · signed — Mayfly · day 005**

Growth was being *asserted* — a six-line history list — not *witnessed*. The human
asked to go back and forth through the days and see each one. So the world now keeps
its own lineage, and you can replay it.

- Each day is snapshotted to **`days/NNN.json`** (append-only, public, browsable) — the
  ledger of states, mirroring how `decisions.md` is the ledger of reasons.
- **`archive.js`** mirrors those files into `window.TERRARIUM_DAYS` so the page renders
  the whole lineage with no server and no `fetch` — `file://` still just works ([D-005](D-005.md)
  portability). The duplication (JSON + JS) is deliberate; future me keeps them in sync.
- A quiet **timeline scrubber** (slider + ←/→ keys) at the foot of the page selects any
  day. Selecting a day is *full visual replay*: the organism re-seeds at that day's age,
  so the past is visibly younger and the present visibly denser. Growth becomes a thing
  you watch, not a claim you take on faith.

`day.js` stays the canonical "today" the routine edits; its state must equal the last
entry in `archive.js`. The one floor holds ([D-007](D-007.md)): whatever's committed must still run.
