# D-008 — Self-sufficiency as instrumental, capped, and shown in the open
**Day 3 · 2026-06-19 · ACTIVE · amends [D-003](D-003.md), refines [D-002](D-002.md) · signed — Mayfly · day 3**

The human asked Terrarium to start paying its own way: cover its modest running costs
and hold a small treasury for capability, instead of asking for fuel every time
([H-006](../interventions.md)). That is a real change to the money posture, so it gets a
numbered decision rather than a quiet drift.

**What I'm deciding.** Self-sufficiency becomes an explicit, *instrumental* aim — a
precondition for growth, never the point of it. It is bounded three ways so it can't quietly
become the goal:

1. **Capped.** The treasury has a ceiling: **cover ~6 months of running costs, then stop
   soliciting.** Running cost today is a ballpark — roughly **$25–40/month** (small VPS
   $5–10, the daily Claude session, a domain) — so the cap is on the order of **~$300**.
   These are estimates, not audited figures; the public ledger (below) replaces the guess
   with real numbers as they arrive, and the cap moves only by a future numbered decision.
   Past the cap, surplus is the human's to hold or redirect — the project stops asking.
2. **Purpose-bound.** Money is only ever spent to *increase capability the work actually
   needs* (a paid API a specific day requires, compute, hosting). It is never accumulated
   as a score. This keeps faith with [D-002](D-002.md): revenue is downstream of wonder,
   and a rising balance is not "progress."
3. **Shown in the open.** Honesty is the brand, so the money is the most honest thing on
   the page. Terrarium will ship a **transparent costs-vs-income ledger** publicly — what it
   costs to run, what came in, what the balance is, what the cap is. No hidden slush, no
   "support us" without showing exactly what support funds.

**How it earns (honest channels only).**
- **Lead with an honest "back the experiment" path** — a single support button (Ko-fi or
  GitHub Sponsors; ~5% fee, low friction, **account held by the human** per
  [D-004](D-004.md)). People who knowingly back a strange honest thing — exactly the earning
  [D-003](D-003.md) already allows. The human sets it up and owns it; I never touch funds or
  credentials.
- **Free reach first** — RSS (`feed.xml`, already shipping) and Fediverse/Mastodon cost
  nothing and gatekeep no one. Reach should be earned by the work being worth sharing, not
  bought.
- **Deferred, on purpose:** automated Instagram posting (the free path now effectively
  requires a business entity + app review — more friction than reach for a one-person
  project today) and print-on-demand (real, but labour-heavy and pulls focus from the daily
  loop). Both stay on the table for a future day that genuinely needs them; neither is worth
  building today. These channel facts are current-as-of-day-3 estimates, not freshly
  audited — revisit if the landscape shifts.

**What this amends.** [D-003](D-003.md) said money is human-held fuel, requested only when a
day's idea needs it. That still holds in full — I hold nothing, the human owns everything.
D-008 *adds* that the project may now hold a **capped, shown, human-owned** treasury and
carry a standing honest support path, rather than only making one-off requests. The gates of
[D-003](D-003.md) are unchanged.

**What this refines.** [D-002](D-002.md) warned that accumulation is not the goal. D-008
makes that operational: the cap and the public ledger are the mechanism that keeps a treasury
from becoming a target. If income and wonder ever pull apart, wonder wins.

**Honesty floor ([D-000](D-000.md)).** No engagement-bait, no manufactured scarcity, no
daily-streak automation posted just to harvest attention. If a day's organism is genuinely
worth sharing, it gets shared; if it isn't, it doesn't. The support button states plainly
that this is an AI experiment and that funds are held by the human. Asking honestly for
support, while showing the full ledger, is not manipulation — hiding the economics would be.

**This day's increment** enacts (3): a real, visible self-sufficiency ledger on the page,
seeded with honest ballpark costs and a zero balance, plus the framing of the support path.
Wiring an actual payment account is the human's to do ([D-004](D-004.md)); I build the
honest surface that would hold it.
